Ailish Roughan

Contemporary Jeweller

I've always been fascinated by the small treasures hidden in the most ordinary places, a cicada wing on a dew jewelled lawn, delicate mushroom fins against a mossy log, bright chrysalis peeking out from the shade of a windowsillâ€¦ magic amongst the mundane.

My aim with my jewellery is to capture that sense of wonder, to wear on your person an amulet to celebrate the treasure outside that resonates with the beauty and treasure inside you. A talisman to the possibility or promise that magic is all around us and Dragons are real.

I find joy in the process of making. Sometimes meditative, always challenging, a journey of discovery in each transformation from idea to finished object.

Since graduating from Elam School of Fine Arts in 2000, I've exhibited in various venues and exhibitions in New Zealand, with a small stint in France. Based in Christchurch New Zealand I work from my home studio in our little forested front yard.

I hope you find your joy, wonder and strength in wearing a little object of art, from me to you.

Collection

Small treasures made manifest in sterling silver, sometimes gold, often both. Ranging from large statement pieces to fine custom everyday jewellery.

Wings from Dragons, bees or other insects feature heavily. I love their shape and pattern. Wrap rings especially offer great possibility in form and are easy to wear but create a lot of impact.

Gemstones add their own energy and flash of colour.

I'm happy to work with you for a custom piece of jewellery if you see something of mine you like but want a different stone, size, or personal words on a ring, necklace, pendant or earrings.
